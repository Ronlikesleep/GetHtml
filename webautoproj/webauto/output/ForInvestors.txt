Buy a Fund To buy a fund, you first need to deposit some USDC to your fund account, which is separate from your perpetual trading account. Alternatively, you can transfer funds from your perpetual account to the funds account. You may access either function in the top-right corner. Currently, Aboard only support USDC for the asset management system. After depositing USDC, you can buy funds on the dedicated fund page. Monitor your Shares After purchasing, you can see how your funds perform via the fund page or your fund dashboard on the asset management page.. Sell your Shares You can always sell your shares via the Fund or Personal Dashboards. Your funds are subject to different lock-up periods depending on the fund terms and purchase time. You can schedule the earliest date you'd like to get your funds back. You are still exposed to the fund's performance (subject to win or loss) until the final delivery date.
